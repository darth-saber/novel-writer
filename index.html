Writing Tips

"Show, don't tell" - Use sensory details and actions instead of simply stating emotions.

Strong dialogue reveals character. Each character should have a unique voice.

Conflict is the engine of story. Every scene should have tension or move the plot forward.

Write with all five senses. Don't just describe what characters see.

Kill your darlings. Be willing to cut even beautiful sentences that don't serve the story.
